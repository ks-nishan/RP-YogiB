Frequently Asked Questions


Do I need special equipment for yoga practice?
Hardly. All you need is a nonslip yoga mat that is long enough to lay fully stretched on your back.


How often should I practice meditation?
Meditation can be practiced every day. Beginners can target 5-10 mins a day, and those with more experience can work up to 30-60 mins twice a day or more.

How are Yoga Asanas different from exercise?
Exercises focus more on physical strength and the ability to do strenuous tasks. Regular exercise helps you develop greater control over your movements and range of motion.


What are the benefits of yoga?
Practising yoga has loads of physical benefits: it creates a flexible, toned and strong body and improves breathing, energy and metabolism.
