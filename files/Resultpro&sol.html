Research Problem & Sollution

Proposed Problem

How can real-time technology be effectively utilized to assist individuals in improving yoga practice, and what are the potential benefits and challenges associated with such assistance?


Research Sollution

>

The research project aims to suggest an effective and accurate yoga asana based on user requests, recognizing the physical and mental benefits of different yoga asanas and adapting them to meet individual needs. The real-time yoga assistant will assess the user's posture and movement using computer vision and machine learning techniques to suggest areas that need attention. The goal is to improve health and well-being by supporting safer and more effective yoga practice. Technology improvements are crucial for making yoga more effective, personalized, and accessible to a wider range of users, including beginners and those with unique health issues. The real-time yoga assistant will need increased precision, accuracy, and overall efficiency.
