The system uses voice-activated yoga asana suggestions, allowing users to express their health-related concerns or preferences. It uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to understand and recommend suitable asanas.

The system tracks and visualizes user performance in yoga sessions, analyzing asana types, durations, and progress. It provides visual representations in graph, enabling users to monitor progress, identify trends, and make informed decisions about their routines and goals.
